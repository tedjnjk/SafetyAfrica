Comprehensive Safety & Risk Management Solutions

Our Safety & Risk Management services provide comprehensive solutions to identify, assess, and mitigate workplace hazards while ensuring compliance with relevant regulations and standards.

  • ISO Certifications

    Support for ISO 14001, ISO 9001, and OHSAS 18001 certification and compliance.

  • Legal & Regulatory Compliance

    Comprehensive compliance management and documentation services.

  • Medical Screening & Surveillance

    DOL Registered medical screening and health surveillance programs.

  • Incident Management

    Professional incident investigation, reporting, and prevention strategies.

  • HSESQ Risk Assessment

    Comprehensive health, safety, environmental, and quality risk assessments.

  • Health & Safety Services

    Including auditing, training certification (SETA Accredited), and regular inspections.

  • Safety Management Planning

    Development and implementation of comprehensive safety management plans.

  • Security Systems

    Electronic safety and security system implementation and maintenance.

  • Fire Safety Systems

    Fire protection and prevention systems design and implementation.

  • Contractor Management

    Construction regulations, NBR, OHS Act compliance, safety files and management.

  • Consumer Protection & Compliance

    CPA compliance, Certificate of Acceptability (COA), and Business Licenses.

  • Food Safety Management

    FSMS implementation (HACCP, ISO 22000:2005) and certification support.

  • Specialized Safety Services

    HAZOP & HAZMAT analysis, ergonomics assessments, and web-based HSESQ management systems.
