About Viable Safe-T-Systems Africa Ltd (VSTSA)

We specialize in designing, implementing, and managing comprehensive Health, Safety, Environmental, and Quality (HSESQ) management systems.

  • Over 25 years of industry expertise in safety management solutions
  • Comprehensive compliance with legal, statutory, and regulatory requirements
  • Qualified safety consultants delivering customized solutions including fire safety, risk management, hazardous substances handling, and occupational safety

Through our innovative Online Management System, we offer real-time updates and ensure seamless record-keeping to keep your operations compliant and efficient. At VSTSA, professionalism, reliability, and excellence are at the core of our service. Together, we build safer workplaces and drive sustainable success.

HSESQ Auditing & Compliance

Comprehensive audits and assessments of your HSESQ standards, including internal/external audits, legal compliance assessments, and risk management evaluations.

HSESQ Management Systems

Tailored systems meeting ISO standards (45001, 14001, 9001) with cloud-based compliance tracking solutions for streamlined safety and environmental processes.

Fire Safety Services

Complete fire safety solutions including risk assessments, evacuation planning, equipment servicing, and fire suppression system design and installation.

Hazardous Substances Management

Expert guidance for handling hazardous materials, including chemical risk assessments, training programs, and HazCom plan development.

Workplace Safety Training

Comprehensive training programs including HSESQ awareness, emergency response, and hazard identification workshops.

Incident Investigation

Thorough incident investigations with root cause analysis and preventive action planning to ensure workplace safety.
